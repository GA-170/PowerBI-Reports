**🚑 Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard (Power BI)******

**📌 Project Overview**

This project analyzes emergency room data to understand patient flow, wait times, and operational efficiency. The goal was to move beyond static reporting and build an interactive dashboard that answers key business questions around patient experience and hospital performance.

The dataset spans ~19 months and includes 9,000+ patient records.

**🎯 Problem Statement**

Emergency rooms often face challenges such as:

Long patient wait times
Uneven workload across hours and days
Limited visibility into referrals and patient demographics

This project focuses on answering:

When is the ER most overloaded?
Are patients being seen within the 30-minute SLA?
What are the key drivers of patient volume?

**🛠️ Tools & Technologies**
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Power Query (Data Transformation)
Excel / CSV (Data Source)

**📊 Dashboard Features**
1. Monthly View
Patient volume trends
Average wait time
Satisfaction score
Referral patterns
2. Consolidated View
Overall KPIs across entire dataset
SLA tracking (% patients seen within 30 minutes)
Day vs Hour heatmap for peak load analysis
3. Patient Details
Drill-down table with patient-level data
Filters for deeper analysis
4. Key Takeaways
Summary of insights for business users


**🔧 Data Processing & Modeling**
Cleaned inconsistent date/time formats
Extracted hour-level granularity for time-based analysis
Built calculated columns and measures using DAX
Optimized model by removing unnecessary columns


**📈 Key Insights**
Average wait time: ~35 minutes
Only ~60% of patients are seen within 30 minutes
Peak load occurs during evenings and Mondays
Most referrals go to General Practice and Orthopedics
Nearly equal distribution between admitted and non-admitted patients
